People

At its core, Custo is defined by its people. We welcome talent of all backgrounds and from all over the world to create unforgettable games and bring to life ideas that would otherwise be impossible. passion

To grant our teams the freedom to create new experiences and bring their diverse perspectives to life, we remain committed to our independence as a game-maker and publisher. Custo’s teams have fought – and won – to preserve this independence so we can chart our own future and uphold our values of creativity and embracing the unexpected. Our teams and stakeholders know that our freedom is paramount to our ongoing success.

Code of Conduct

Whether they apply to our employees, our players, or our business partners, Ubisoft's Codes of Conduct are reference documents that define our commitments as a company and game publisher. At Ubisoft we are committed to high ethical standards and to providing a safe, respectful and inclusive work or gaming environment for everyone in our global community.

Our Team

We are a company of enthusiasts who are eager to share, collaborate and innovate in order to make a positive impact on the future of gaming. Our team members come from all over the world, have different backgrounds and are passionate about solving exciting challenges every day. We are the successful studio we are today thanks to the unparalleled dedication and support of our team. Diverse minds coming together can accomplish daring goals that would be unachievable alone. We are individually driven by an ambition to make amazing games. Each of us is empowered by our expertise to innovate on our games, processes, and culture. That drive and ownership is strengthened by the support of our team, spurring our ambition to deliver our best work.
